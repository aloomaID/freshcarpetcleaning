Fairview Park, OH Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Fairview Park, a picturesque city in Cuyahoga County, Ohio, lies adjacent to Cleveland. It once formed part of Rockport Township, along with West Park, Lakewood, and Rocky River. It borders Cleveland to the east, Westlake and North Olmsted to the west, and Rocky River and Lakewood to the north, with Brook Park to the south. At the 2020 census, the city population was 17,291.
